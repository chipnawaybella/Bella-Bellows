“I love to sing!” ”I can hear music in my head and enjoy singing at my window before I go to sleep at night.”  Her voice softens, “I sing to the stars, and pretend they are real people.”  

One day the friends sat down for a picnic in the park. They listened to Bella’s share her dreams. She dreamed of being strong enough to take a deep breath and sing to an audience in the park under the trees. Ben leaned forward and said, “We all have dreams that we want to come true.” “And you will sing for all of us one day, Bella.” 

Months after Ben’s championship win, and Felipe began his soccer tryouts, the town gathered for the special event in the park.
